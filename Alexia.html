Computer Technology: Using Websites

Today's Topic: Video Games and Inclusivity

1.1 Read the Overview Text

Overview: Blizzard Entertainment is a company that makes popular video games like World of Warcraft, Diablo, and Overwatch. The company is trying to include ideas from different types of people in their games. This means thinking about people of different cultures, ages, genders, and people with disabilities.

1.2 We Will Use This Website

We will look at the Blizzard company website: https://www.blizzard.com/

This website is for selling games. Company information is often on a different page, like an "About" page. Our activities will help us find it.

Activity 1: Warm-Up Questions

Read the Overview Text in Section 1.1 again. Find the answers to these questions. You can write your answers in the box.
